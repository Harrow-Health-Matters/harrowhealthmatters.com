Laura is a **yoga teacher** and **Bowen therapist** with over 30 years of experience supporting people on their journey towards greater health, wellbeing, and self-understanding. Her work is rooted in a deep appreciation of the body's natural capacity to heal, adapt, and find balance.

Drawing on decades of life experience, study, and practice, Laura offers a gentle yet profound approach that helps people reconnect with themselves through movement, breath, awareness, and therapeutic touch. Specialising in Hatha Yoga for the over-50s, she combines yoga, meditation, breathwork, and Bowen Therapy to support strength, mobility, balance, and overall well being.

Whether helping someone regain confidence in movement, navigate physical challenges, or find greater peace of mind, Laura's approach is compassionate, individual, and holistic. Students and clients are drawn to her calm presence and her belief that true wellbeing arises when body, mind, and spirit are given the opportunity to work in harmony.

If you're ready to explore a gentler, more connected approach to health and wellbeing, Laura welcomes you to take the next step. Whether you're interested in yoga classes, Bowen Therapy, retreats, or personalised one-to-one support, she would be delighted to help you find the path that best suits your needs.
